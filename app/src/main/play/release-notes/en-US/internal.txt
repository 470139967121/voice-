Apple Sign-In helper via ASAuthorizationController (PR D) (#335)
- Creates performAppleSignIn() suspend function that:
- - Triggers ASAuthorizationController with email + name scopes
- - Generates cryptographic nonce (SecRandomCopyBytes)
- - Hashes nonce with CC_SHA256 for Apple's nonce requirement
- - Returns AppleSignInResult(idToken, rawNonce) for AuthViewModel
